Cycle Deviations

Help women facilitate meaningful conversations with their doctors through interactive visualization.

A sneak peek into how we can visualize data

What is a Cycle Deviation?

Project Aim

Currently, the cycle deviations page lacks visual content, making it challenging for users to fully understand key concepts. This summer, my goal was to enhance the page by adding contextualized information to the prolonged, irregular, spotting, and infrequent deviation sections. These improvements aim to empower users with clearer insights, enabling them to engage in more meaningful and productive conversations with their doctors.

Thank you for making a product that empowers women to drive meaningful medical conversations.
Data is meaningful. I want other women to feel as empowered as I have felt - to use the right words, convey facts and be heard.
