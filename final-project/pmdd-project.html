PMDD Visualization Project

Empowering women worldwide by making PMDD data actionable and accessible through interactive visualization.

A sneak peek into our visualization interface designed to assist in PMDD self-diagnosis.

What is PMDD?

Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder (PMDD) is a severe form of premenstrual syndrome (PMS) that significantly impacts mental health. It affects around 31 million women globally, leading to mood swings, depression, and other emotional and physical symptoms.

Project Aim

This project aims to assist users in self-diagnosing PMDD through interactive data visualization. By logging moods and symptoms over time, the system detects patterns in mood swings, anxiety, irritability, and other premenstrual symptoms. If these patterns match PMDD criteria across multiple cycles, users are alerted about a potential diagnosis. This promotes early awareness and self-care.
