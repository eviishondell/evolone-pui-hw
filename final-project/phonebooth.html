PhoneBooth App

Building connections through voice-based interactions and shared interests. Explore the art of persuasive design.

The PhoneBooth App fosters meaningful relationships through voice and shared passions.

Project Aim

The PhoneBooth App was designed to foster meaningful connections between users based on shared interests through voice-based interactions. The app emphasizes persuasive design elements such as distancing and self-awareness to encourage deeper and more authentic relationships.
